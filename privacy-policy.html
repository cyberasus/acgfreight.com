ACG Freight Inc. (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the information to:
Send you operational, promotional, and informational texts

Comply with applicable laws and industry standards
Prevent fraud, spam, or misuse of the service

4. Opt-In and Opt-Out Procedures
Opt-In:
You may subscribe by:
Texting START or YES to 773-359-1509
Signing up on our website

Opt-Out:
You may opt out at any time by replying with any of the following keywords:
STOP, CANCEL, END, QUIT, UNSUBSCRIBE, STOPALL to any message you receive
Customer Support
For assistance, reply with HELP or INFO, or contact us at admin@acgfreight.com
